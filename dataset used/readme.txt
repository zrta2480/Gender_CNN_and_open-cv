Dataset used:
Titel of folder: gender_rev2
Description:
The second revision of a dataset containing 1747 female face images and 1747 male images as training data. 100 images in each gender in both testing and validation data.

Source of dataset:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gpiosenka/gender-classification-from-an-image

Author of dataset:
